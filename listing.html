About this property

A beautifully maintained Queenslander on a quiet street in the heart of New Farm, one block from the riverside parklands and walking distance to Merthyr Village. The home retains all original character — VJ walls, timber floors, pressed tin ceilings — while offering a fully updated kitchen and two renovated bathrooms.

The north-facing rear deck looks over a private tropical garden, perfect for entertaining year-round. The main bedroom features a generous walk-in robe and ensuite. A separate study makes for an ideal home office. The property sits on a 405m² block with rear lane access and a single lock-up garage.

New Farm Ferry Terminal is a 4-minute walk. The CBD is 10 minutes by ferry or 15 minutes by bike. This is the kind of home that comes to market once in a decade on this street.
